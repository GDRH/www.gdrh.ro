 Want to join our team?

Here are a few simple steps to follow in order to enter GDRH

Step 1

Complete this form. Your application will be analyzed by our team as soon as possible and a decision will be made. We will try to keep the same level for the requirements.

Step 2

In case your application is accepted, we will contact you and you will be added in the closed group on Facebook, the group on Google and the Github organization.

Step 3

Welcome to GDRH! (Tip: it's mandatory to come up with an idea for a new project or to enter a team working on an already existing project, these are found on the Github link below.)
